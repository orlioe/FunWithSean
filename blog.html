Orlin Uzunov

My name is Orlin Uzunov. I signed for this course, because of my previous hobby work in creating websites. Pretty amateur at that time (early 2000), just moving pictures around with Dreamweaver, no coding. Other then that I have a mechanical engineering education and work experience in networking, automotive, maintaining critical equipment in industry.

My hobby is expirimenting with stuff in Operating systems, performance modifications of cars, cycling, hiking and spend time with my family.
